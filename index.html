Frequently Asked Questions

1. How long can I keep the dumpster on my site?

Our standard rental periods are flexible, usually ranging from 7 to 14 days, though we can accommodate longer durations based on project needs.

2. What items are prohibited in the roll off containers?

We cannot accept hazardous waste, tires, batteries, or liquids like wet paint and oil.

3. Does someone need to be present for delivery?

As long as the drop-off area is clear and accessible, you do not need to be on-site for the delivery or pickup.

4. How much weight can the 20-yard dumpster hold?

Most 20-yard bins have a weight limit of 2 to 3 tons, which is ideal for standard household or renovation debris.

5. Can the dumpster damage my driveway?

We recommend placing plywood down for protection, and our drivers are trained to place containers with the utmost care.
